🎅 About the Event

Join us for a festive, friendly short walk through the wintery forest with fellow furries and therians! We’ll walk to a nearby calm location featuring a meadow, benches, and space to chill, take photos, and connect. We’ll also be hosting an optional Secret Santa exchange! Just bring a wrapped gift worth under 10 € to join in the fun. Whether you’re new to the community or returning from past events, this is a cozy way to celebrate the season with like-minded friends!

🎁 What to Bring

  • Optional Secret Santa gift (under 10€)
  • Snacks, water, warm clothes
  • Good vibes only — no alcohol, weapons, or drama!

❓ FAQ

Is the event free?

Yes! But registration is required.

Can I bring over-18 friends?

No, this event is strictly for under-18 attendees.

Yes, as long as you're comfortable and it’s appropriate for walking outside!

What if I’m late?

Please arrive between 12:00–12:30. No late entry will be allowed.

What if I need help during the event?

Ask one of the organisers or helpers - we’re here for you!
