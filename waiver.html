2. Legal Guardian Declaration

The legal guardian declares that:

  • They are the parent or legal guardian of the minor participant
  • They have given consent for the minor to participate in Furosion events
  • They are informed about the nature of the event, its course, and rules
  • They are responsible for the minor participant’s health, behaviour, and readiness

3. Nature of the Event

  • Events take place outdoors or in public spaces.
  • Events do not provide medical care or insurance.

4. Liability Waiver

The legal guardian acknowledges and agrees that:

  • Participation of the minor is voluntary and at their own risk
  • Furosion o.z., organizers, and volunteers are not responsible for injuries, illnesses, losses, or damages
  • The organizer is not responsible for the behaviour of other participants or third parties
  • The organizer is not responsible for loss or damage to personal belongings

5. Health and Safety

  • The legal guardian confirms that the minor is medically fit to participate in the event.
  • In case of health limitations, it is the guardian’s responsibility to inform the organizers.
  • Organizers do not replace the supervision provided by the legal guardian.

6. Behaviour and Organizer Instructions

  • The minor participant must follow the event rules and instructions of the organizers.
  • Violation of rules may result in immediate removal from the event.
  • The legal guardian acknowledges that in case of serious violations, they may be contacted.

7. Photography and Video

  • Photos and videos may be taken during the event.
  • They are used for documentation and presentation of Furosion o.z. activities.
  • If the legal guardian does not wish the minor to be recorded, they must notify the organizers or ensure avoidance of areas being photographed.

8. Final Provisions

This declaration is legally binding and applies to all Furosion events unless stated otherwise. The current version of the document is available at furosion.com.
