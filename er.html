Furosion Event Rules

Version: 2025.1 — These rules apply to all in-person Furosion-branded events unless explicitly stated otherwise on the official event page at furosion.com.

1. Attendee Age

  • All participants must be under 18 on the day of the event. No exceptions.
  • The suggested minimum age is 8. Children younger than this are discouraged due to safety, stamina, and maturity requirements.

2. Clothing & Dress Code

  • Furry- and therian-themed apparel is encouraged: tails, ears, masks, animal-inspired outfits, etc.
  • Partial or full fursuits are allowed but not recommended for forest or muddy terrain due to damage and safety risks.
  • All attire must be age-appropriate, non-sexualized, and safe for movement and visibility.

3. Safety and Group Conduct

  • No yelling, running, throwing objects, pushing, climbing, or dangerous behavior.
  • No engaging with members of the public without their consent. Shouting or making contact with strangers is banned.
  • Romantic affection such as hand-holding or hugging is allowed. Any sexual behavior, including groping or graphic discussion, is prohibited.
  • All participants must follow the lead of the group, remain within safe distance of staff, and notify someone if they intend to leave early.

4. Forbidden Items

  • Do not bring: alcohol, drugs, vapes, cigarettes, fireworks, weapons (real or replica), pornography, or anything illegal under Slovak or international law.
  • Organisers reserve the right to inspect bags or belongings and prohibit any item without explanation.
  • Only certified service animals are permitted. Pets are not allowed due to risk of injury or being stepped on.

5. Photography and Consent

  • Photos and videos will be taken throughout the event by organisers and attendees.
  • By attending, you automatically consent to appearing in public-facing media. If you do not wish to be photographed, do not enter areas where cameras are active.
  • No request to opt-out, blur, or remove photos will be accepted.

6. Health Guidelines

  • If you are ill, especially with contagious symptoms or a positive COVID-19 test in the past 15 days, do not attend.
  • Minor health needs (first aid kits, tissues, water) are encouraged, but the event does not provide medical personnel or liability coverage.
  • Notify a volunteer or organiser if you feel unwell. The organisers are not responsible for medical outcomes.

7. Personal Items

  • You may bring water, snacks, a bag, plushies, gifts, art, or other safe items.
  • Do not bring valuables. Furosion is not responsible for lost, damaged, or stolen property.
  • Item trading and sharing is allowed, but not enforced or guaranteed safe.

8. Volunteers

  • Volunteers are selected in advance and are all under 21 but aged 13 or older.
  • Volunteers may remind attendees of rules, assist with navigation, carry group items, and help organisers with safety checks.
  • Volunteers may not enforce bans or expel participants — only core organisers can do so.

9. Violations and Removal

  • Breaking any rule may result in being asked to leave immediately.
  • If a participant refuses to leave when asked, they will be permanently blacklisted from future events, and their parents or guardians may be contacted.
  • Failure to comply with exit requests may result in local authorities being notified for trespassing or public endangerment.

10. Disclaimers

  • Attendance is entirely voluntary and at your own risk. You acknowledge that the event is unofficial and not hosted by a legal entity.
  • Furosion does not carry insurance or medical licensing and assumes no liability for harm, illness, injury, or loss.
  • Attendees are expected to behave with maturity, common sense, and kindness. All decisions by staff are final.
