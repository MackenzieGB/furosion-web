Furosion o.z. – Event Rules for Participants Under 18

Version: 2.2026 — These rules apply to all events organised by Furosion o.z., unless stated otherwise at furosion.com.

Legal Information: Furosion o.z.,
Company ID: pending,
Address: Tomášikova 45/A, Bratislava – Nové Mesto, 831 04, Slovak Republic.

1. Participant Age

  • All participants must be under 18 years old on the day of the event. No exceptions.
  • The recommended minimum age is 8 years for safety and maturity reasons.
  • Registration for participants under 18 must be completed by a parent or legal guardian. Minors cannot register themselves.

2. Clothing and Dress Code

  • Furry or therian-themed clothing is recommended: tails, ears, masks, costumes, etc.
  • Fursuits (full or partial) are allowed but not recommended in forested or muddy areas due to safety and costume protection.
  • All clothing must be age-appropriate, non-sexualised, and safe for movement and visibility.

3. Safety and Behaviour

  • No shouting, running, throwing objects, pushing, climbing, or otherwise endangering oneself or others.
  • Do not approach or interact with the public without consent.
  • Safe, age-appropriate romantic gestures like holding hands or hugging are allowed. Any sexual behaviour is strictly prohibited.
  • Participants must follow instructions from organisers and volunteers and inform them if planning to leave early.

4. Prohibited Items

  • Do not bring or use: alcohol, drugs, vaporizers, cigarettes, fireworks, weapons (real or replicas), pornography, or any illegal items.
  • Organisers may inspect bags and prohibit any item at their discretion.
  • Only certified assistance animals are allowed. Pets are not permitted for safety reasons.

5. Photography and Video

  • Organisers and participants may take photos and videos during the event.
  • By attending, the participant and their parent/legal guardian agree to the use of these materials on websites, social media, and other media without compensation.
  • Registration data (name, email, age) is processed according to GDPR and used exclusively for communication and event safety.

6. Health and Safety

  • Participants must not attend if sick or contagious, or if they tested positive for COVID-19 within the last 15 days.
  • Organisers do not provide medical care or insurance. Participants are responsible for their own health and safety.
  • In case of injury or health issues, the participant or parent/legal guardian must immediately notify an organiser.

7. Personal Belongings

  • Participants may bring water, snacks, bags, plushies, or other safe items.
  • Furosion o.z. is not responsible for lost, damaged, or stolen personal items.

8. Volunteers and Organisers

  • Volunteers are pre-selected and aged 13–21.
  • They assist with navigation, safety oversight, and reminding participants of rules.
  • Only main organisers may decide to remove a participant from an event.

9. Rule Violations

  • Breaking the rules may lead to immediate removal from the event.
  • Failure to follow instructions may result in bans from future events and notification of the parent/legal guardian.

10. Liability Disclaimer

  • Participation is voluntary and at the participant's own risk. Events are organised by Furosion o.z., which is not liable for injuries, illness, or property loss.
  • Parents or legal guardians are responsible for supervising their child and ensuring compliance with these rules.

11. Parental/Guardian Consent

  • Registration of participants under 18 must be completed by a parent or legal guardian.
  • The parent confirms they have read the rules, agree with them, and accept all risks associated with the child’s participation.

Questions or feedback can be sent to support@furosion.com.
