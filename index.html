FAQ

Who can attend?

This outing is exclusively designed for those under 18—young furries and therians only.

How do I register?

Select your ticket, confirm your age, and follow the instructions!

What should I bring?

Bring enough water, some snacks, and a light raincoat just in case! Please stay hydrated!!

Is the location safe?

Yes, but we're not responsible for your safety if you don't listen to the organisers.
